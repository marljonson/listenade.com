Immunity
Clairo
2019
7.6


Clairo's Immunity is a quietly confident exploration of indie pop's emotional range. Tracks like “Bags” and “Sofia” balance lo-fi charm with polished production, where her soft vocals cut through like a cool breeze. The introspective lyrics touch on love, identity, and vulnerability, but without wallowing in self-pity. A little fragile, a little grounded, it's a triumph in low-key tone and texture.

Favorite song: "Alewife"
